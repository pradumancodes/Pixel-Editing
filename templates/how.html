How to Use

  1. Select an image you want to edit.
  2. Choose the type of operation you want to perform (e.g., Convert to PNG, Convert to Grayscale, etc.).
  3. Click the "Submit" button to upload the image and apply the chosen operation.
  4. Once processed, the edited image will be available for download.
